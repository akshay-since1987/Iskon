The Temple Hall

A spiritual sanctum crafted in pure white marble, the temple hall is the heart of ISKCON Dhule. It houses the magnificent deities of Sri Sri Radha Rasabihari, Sri Sri Sita Rama Laxman Hanuman, and Sri Sri Gaura Nitai.

Govinda's Restaurant

Govinda’s is a pure vegetarian restaurant that specializes in Indian, Continental, and Chinese cuisine served as sanctified Prasadam. It offers a calm, spiritual dining experience for all visitors.
