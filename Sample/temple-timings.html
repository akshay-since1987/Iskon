Temple Timings

Experience the divine presence according to the daily schedule.

Arati Schedule

Ritual Time Description
Mangala Arati 04:30 AM The first arati of the day, waking up the Lord with blissful prayers.
Tulsi Arati 05:00 AM Worship of the holy Tulsi plant for spiritual growth.
Greeting of Deities 07:15 AM A beautiful darsana accompanied by the singing of Govindam prayers.
Srila Prabhupada Guru Puja 07:30 AM Paying homage to the founder-acharya of ISKCON.
Srimad Bhagavatam Class 08:00 AM Daily spiritual discourse from the Srimad Bhagavatam.
Dhupa Arati 08:30 AM Incense offering accompanied by kirtan.
Raj Bhoga Arati 12:30 PM The major arati followed by a grand offering of food.

Evening Schedule

Ritual Time Description
Dhupa Arati 04:15 PM Opening darsana in the evening.
Tulsi Arati 06:45 PM Evening worship of the Tulsi plant.
Sandhya Arati (Gaura Arati) 07:00 PM The grand evening arati with enthusiastic congregational kirtan.
Bhagavad Gita Class 08:00 PM Evening spiritual lecture on the teachings of Krishna.
Shayana Arati 08:30 PM The final arati before the deities retire for the night.

Important Note:

The temple hall remains closed for the deities' rest from 1:00 PM to 4:15 PM and after 9:00 PM. Please plan your visit accordingly to ensure you have a peaceful darsana.
